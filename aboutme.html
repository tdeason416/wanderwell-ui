WanderWell


Travis R. Deason

Data Scientist

Hi, I'm a Data Scientist with a Mechanical Engineer background living in Seattle, WA. About a year ago I started to dig deep into Data Science because I felt I could better apply my quantitative and analysis skills.

I love travelling but like most Americans have limited time off. I believe when you have just a few days in a city, you can't afford to waste time. Travel and review sites are nice, but they don't give you a feel of what you're looking for in a city. I've created WanderWell to help travellers make data-driven travel decisions.
